The Journey of Rana Anshuman Singh

Born in Varanasi, the spiritual heart of India, Rana Anshuman Singh, known creatively as Ansh Sisodia, is a storyteller working across cinema, poetry and digital media. He began filmmaking at 18 and gained national recognition for his award-winning short film Soch-Aalay. With a bachelor’s and a master’s degree in Mass Communication from Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith University, he blends academic rigor with instinctive artistic expression.

A passionate content creator and digital media professional, Ansh specialises in creative writing, social media strategy, SEO, graphic design and voice narration. His experience spans roles at Unified Bharat, Enrich IAS and MGKVP, where he led content, campaigns and media coordination. Writing in Hindi and Urdu across poetry and narrative forms, he crafts emotionally resonant stories that engage, inspire and drive meaningful impact.
